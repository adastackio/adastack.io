# Introduction Resources

Resources that provide an introduction to the Cardano network and ecosystem.

### Official Links
- [Cardano.org](https://cardano.org/): The official Cardano website.  
- [Official Cardano Roadmap](https://roadmap.cardano.org/en/): The 5 phase roadmap for Cardano's development.  
- [IOG Blog](https://iohk.io/blog/): Blog of Input Output Global, the main company behind Cardano's development. 
- [Cardano Foundation](https://cardanofoundation.org/blog): Blog of the Cardano Foundation. Check here for announcements, events, and partnerships.  
- [IOG Research Library](https://iohk.io/en/research/library/): Browse the peer-reviewed research behind the Cardano blockchain.  

### Intro Videos
- [Charles Hoskinson Whiteboard](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ja9D0kpksxw): IOG Founder Charles Hoskinson explains Cardano in this popular video from 2017.  
- [How do you Explain the Mission of Cardano](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=l_Nv0-PVrnM): IOG explains the mission of Cardano.  
- [What is Cardano by ThinkGrowCrypto](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BJ6l4oseK_k): Features and properties of Cardano.  
- [Cardano ADA Explained by Exodus](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5vQ5wePI-Fw): 5 minute video looking at Cardano's past and future.  

### Written Guides
- [The Essential Cardano Guide to the Ecosystem](https://services.iohk.io/hubfs/EssentialCardano/PDF/Essential%20Cardano%20Guide%20to%20the%20Ecosystem.pdf?hsLang=en): This guide from the Cardano Foundation discusses Cardano's history, growth, ecosystem, development, and ongoing initiatives.  
- [Reddit Guide to Getting Started with Cardano](https://www.reddit.com/r/cardano/comments/lnj5ne/getting_started_guide_a_newbies_guide_to_cardano/): A Reddit guide to getting started that includes links to various resources.  
- [Introduction to Cardano from the Cardano Docs](https://docs.cardano.org/new-to-cardano/introduction/): Intro section of the official Cardano documentation.  
- [Cardano Wiki by CardanoSpot](https://cardanospot.io/wiki): A guide from Emurgo that includes an overview of Cardano's history, features, and advantages.
