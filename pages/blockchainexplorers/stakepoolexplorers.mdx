# Cardano Stake Pool Explorers

Stake Pool Explorers allow you to browse or search stake pools. Stake pools are the block producers in Cardano and are integral to the security and decentralization of the blockchain. 

#### AdaPools
[AdaPools](https://adapools.org/) is a lightweight, OG cardano explorer with a focus on pool stats. Created by the same team as [Cexplorer](https://cexplorer.io/).

#### PoolTool.io
[PoolTool.io](https://pooltool.io/) is a useful website for pool finding and comparison. They also have cool features such as a mobile app, a pooltoolbot to get alerts about your pool, and a way to select and compare your favorite pools. Stake to [LOVE](https://pooltool.io/pool/95c4956f7a137f7fe9c72f2e831e6038744b6307d00143b2447e6443/epochs) to support the project. 

#### Adafolio.io
[Adafolio.com](https://adafolio.com/) collects community-created stake pools portfolios. The site has useful categories like "Charity Pools" and "Community Developers", useful for choosing a stake pool that aligns with your values.
