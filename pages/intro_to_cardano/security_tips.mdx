# Security Tips

When using cryptocurrency it is very important to take some basic precautions so you can keep your assets safe. Here we link to some resources to stay safe online. 

## General Online Security Guides
These guides provide general tips for staying safe online.

[10 Personal Cyber Security Tips by Cipher](https://cipher.com/blog/10-personal-cyber-security-tips-cyberaware/): A short list of 10 of top tips for staying safe online, including ones relevant to crypto like avoiding phishing attacks.  
[Privacy First Steps by Seth for Privacy](https://sethforprivacy.com/posts/privacy-first-steps/): Tips from security expert Seth about how to get started.  
[Why Privacy Matters from PrivacyGuides.com](https://www.privacyguides.org/en/basics/why-privacy-matters/): The first in a useful series of Guides to privacy.  
[Personal Security Checklist by Lissy93 on Github](https://github.com/Lissy93/personal-security-checklist): A thorough open-source guide with many tips about how to secure your devices, stay safe online, protect your digital assets, and more.  
[EpochSec Security Guides](https://epochsec.io/security-guides/): A series of PDFs from the Cardano security guru EpochSec. They relate to general good practices, staying safe in crypto, securing your computer, and more.  

## Cryptocurrency Security Guides
These guides provide advice for keeping your cryptocurrency safe. 

[How Secure Your Crypto Life, Avoid Scams & Protect Your Crypto Assets by Learn Cardano](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=W2YeB5clm0k): This video from the [Learn Cardano](https://www.youtube.com/@LearnCardano) channel provides advice about how to store your crypto, avoid scams, and use strong authentication.  
[Top Crypto Security Tips by Coinbureau](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=h6m7psfXxzQ): An overview video covering many good practices to keep in mind for crypto security.  
[Security Tips to Keep Your Crypto Life Safe by Learn Cardano](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bRFEx_TNc24): An interview with security expert EpochSec covering ransomware, scams, hacks and general security tips.  
[Cybersecurity Guidelines for Cardano Users by IOG](https://iohk.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/900005141163): A written guide from IOG with advice about downloading wallets, keeping your computer safe, and handling computer repairs.
