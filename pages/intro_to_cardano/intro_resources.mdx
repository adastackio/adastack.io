# Introduction Resources

## Intro Videos
These intro videos explain the Cardano blockchain, how it works, and what is being built on it.

[Kaizen Crypto Cardano 101 Course](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLrbMFdXZnzoAwfxIVo-SQ_qEBCqbwuU6o): A full video series introduction to Cardano for beginners.  
[Charles Hoskinson Whiteboard](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ja9D0kpksxw): Cardano founder Charles Hoskinson explains the ideas behind Cardano in this popular video from 2017.  
[What is Cardano by University of Crypto](https://youtu.be/ctsdCs9IOE4?si=Ozsb0yFzUB7wcq_s): A short 5 minute overview of Cardano and what's being built on it.  
[Genius Academy Cardano Basics Course](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PL9Uhix-1Dfh7yJzg3ZWnIYuo7Ph15VRvN): A short video series from Genius Academy detailing some important concepts in blockchain.  

## Intro Guides
These intro guides give an introduction the Cardano network, its ecosystem, and provide links to various useful resources.

[The Essential Cardano Guide to the Ecosystem](https://services.iohk.io/hubfs/EssentialCardano/PDF/Essential%20Cardano%20Guide%20to%20the%20Ecosystem.pdf?hsLang=en): This very readable guide from the Cardano Foundation discusses Cardano's history, growth, ecosystem, development, and ongoing initiatives.  
[Reddit Guide to Getting Started with Cardano](https://www.reddit.com/r/cardano/comments/lnj5ne/getting_started_guide_a_newbies_guide_to_cardano/): A useful Reddit getting started guide that links to various Cardano resources.
