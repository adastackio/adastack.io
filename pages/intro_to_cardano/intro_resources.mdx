# Introduction Resources

These resources provide an introduction to the Cardano network. 

### Intro Videos
[Charles Hoskinson Whiteboard](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ja9D0kpksxw): IOG Founder Charles Hoskinson explains Cardano in this popular video from 2017.  
[How do you Explain the Mission of Cardano](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=l_Nv0-PVrnM): IOG explains the mission of Cardano.  
[What is Cardano by ThinkGrowCrypto](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BJ6l4oseK_k): Features and properties of Cardano.  
[Cardano ADA Explained by Exodus](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5vQ5wePI-Fw): 5 minute video looking at Cardano's past and future.  

### Written Guides
[The Essential Cardano Guide to the Ecosystem](https://services.iohk.io/hubfs/EssentialCardano/PDF/Essential%20Cardano%20Guide%20to%20the%20Ecosystem.pdf?hsLang=en): This guide from the Cardano Foundation discusses Cardano's history, growth, ecosystem, development, and ongoing initiatives.  
[Reddit Guide to Getting Started with Cardano](https://www.reddit.com/r/cardano/comments/lnj5ne/getting_started_guide_a_newbies_guide_to_cardano/): A Reddit getting started guide that links to various resources.  
[Introduction to Cardano from the Cardano Docs](https://docs.cardano.org/new-to-cardano/introduction/): Intro section of the official Cardano documentation.  
[Cardano Wiki by CardanoSpot](https://cardanospot.io/wiki): A guide from Emurgo that gives an overview of Cardano's history, features, and advantages.
