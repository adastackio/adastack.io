## Cardano Roadmap
Cardano has a well-defined development roadmap with 5 phases. The first three phases are largely complete. Now, development is focused on the final two phases: "Basho" for scaling to handle growing adoption, and "Voltaire" which enacts Cardano's democratic Governance.

[Official Cardano Roadmap](https://roadmap.cardano.org/en/)
