Once you've created and funded a Cardano wallet there are many options to start using the network. Here are some ideas to get started.

### [Stake Your ADA](../staking/start_staking)

Staking ADA allows you to earn around 3% yearly rewards while helping secure the Cardano network. Staked ADA remains in your wallet and under your control.

## Guides to Use the Cardano Network

Here are additional guides and videos from the community to get started on Cardano:

- [Getting On Chain Guide by SelfDriven](https://buildingoncardano.dev/getting-on-chain): This guide explains how to set up a wallet, purchase ADA, send it to your wallet, stake to earn rewards, and begin using dApps.
- [Learn Cardano in 60 Minutes by Crypto Moose](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=T0uik05RW-U): This video walks through how to set up a wallet, stake ADA, buy an NFT, use a DEX, and providing liquidity to earn additional income.
- [Your Cardano Onboarding Guide by Essential Cardano](https://www.essentialcardano.io/article/your-cardano-onboarding-guide): This written guide explores some of the possibilities for what to do after you've set up and funded your wallet.
