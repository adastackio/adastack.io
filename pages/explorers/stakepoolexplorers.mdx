# Cardano Stake Pool Explorers

Stake Pool Explorers allow you to browse or search stake pools.  

- [AdaPools](https://adapools.org/)
- [PoolTool.io](https://pooltool.io/)
- [Adafolio.com](https://adafolio.com/) collects community-created stake pools portfolios. The site has useful categories like "Charity Pools" and "Community Developers". They are also looking ahead to when multi-delegation staking is released and you can stake to multiple pools. [Support](https://adafolio.com/support) Adafolio by staking to [VIPER pool](https://viperstaking.com/).
