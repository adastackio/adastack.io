# Stake Pool Explorers

Stake Pool Explorers to search and view stake pools.

- [Cexplorer Stake Pools](https://cexplorer.io/pool): Rich dashboard to explore pools.
- [AdaStat](https://adastat.net/pools): Rank and search stake pools.
- [PoolTool.io](https://pooltool.io/pools): Search and explore stake pools. There's also a Telegram bot for pool alerts.
- [CardanoScan Stake Pools](https://cardanoscan.io/pools): Pool explorer and stake pool Telegram bot.
- [Adafolio](https://adafolio.com/): Community-created stake pools collections.
- [Stake Pool Alliances](https://www.adastack.io/staking/stake_pool_alliances): Explore collectives of Cardano stake pools.
