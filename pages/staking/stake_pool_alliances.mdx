# Stake Pool Alliances

Collectives of stake pool operators with a similar missions or goals. By joining an alliance, SPOs can attract delegators and support other pools.

- [Armada Alliance](https://armada-alliance.com/)<OS repoURL="https://github.com/armada-alliance" />: A community of energy-efficient stake pool operations.
- [Mission Driven Pools](https://missiondrivenpools.org/)<OS repoURL="https://github.com/CardanoMDP" />: A collective of mission-based stake pools.
- [xSPO Alliance](https://www.xspo-alliance.org/)<OS repoURL="https://github.com/xSPO-Alliance" />: Alliance of small stake pools.
- [Single Pool Alliance](https://singlepoolalliance.net/)<OS repoURL="https://github.com/SinglePoolAlliance" />: Single stake pools promoting diverse decentralization.
- [SPO Japan Guild](https://spojapanguild.net/)<OS repoURL="https://github.com/btbf/spojapanguild" />: Japanese Guild supporting the Japanese community.
- [Canadian Cardano Stake Pool Association](https://ccspa.ca/)<OS repoURL="https://github.com/CCSPA" />: Coalition of Cardano stake pool operators across Canada.
- [Aldea DAO](https://aldea-dao.org/)<OS repoURL="https://github.com/ALDEA-DAO" />: A Latin American DAO with 30+ stake pools supporting local projects.
- [StakePool Operator Tools Alliance](https://github.com/gitmachtl/StakePool-Operator-Tools-Alliance)<OS repoURL="https://github.com/gitmachtl/StakePool-Operator-Tools-Alliance" />: Alliance of stake pool operators developing tools for stake pools.
- [Climate Neutral Cardano](https://climateneutralcardano.org/): Stake pools committed to using 100% renewable energy.
